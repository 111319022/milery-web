1. 資訊收集與使用 (Information Collection and Use)

Milery (「本應用程式」) 非常重視您的隱私。本應用程式的設計宗旨在於幫助使用者追蹤與管理航空哩程。所有的記帳資料與目標設定（包含但不限於哩程數、交易紀錄、航班資訊等）皆透過 Apple 的 SwiftData 技術儲存於您設備的本地端，或透過您的私人 iCloud 帳戶進行同步。開發者「不會」收集、傳輸、儲存或與任何第三方分享您的個人輸入資料。

Milery ("the App") respects your privacy. All your mileage tracking data, flight goals, and transactions are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData technology, or synced securely via your personal iCloud account. The developer does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data with third parties.

The App uses Apple's native frameworks (such as MapKit for displaying flight routes). These native services may collect anonymous device or location information subject to Apple's own Privacy Policy. We do not integrate any third-party ad trackers or analytics tools.
